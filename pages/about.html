What is CSGO starter kit?

CSGO starter kit is a non profit website that aims at providing basic knowledge for those who are interested in playing csgo. Here, you can find weapon performances and descriptions, popular weapon skins, and news and tips about CSGO. With these resources, we hope you can have a pleasant CSGO experience.
